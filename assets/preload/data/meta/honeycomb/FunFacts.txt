An incident once happened that caused humans to be the size of insects, and some of them evolved to have certain insect traits too. Being tiny is very dangerous for humans, but having the survival tactics of insects allows them to stand a chance. A blue-haired rapper guy found himself inside a beehive somehow...
Since Sayori is a regular worker bee, Yuri & Natsuki would also be worker bees as well. Obviously Monika would be the queen bee, because she is best girl.
In the previous version, this song was originally Via Dolce from ARMS, and while there's still elements of it here, it's slightly more altered to be more unique in a way.
According to all known laws of aviation, there is no way that a bee should be able to fly. It's wings are too small to get it's fat little body off the ground. The bee of course flies anyway, because bees don't care what humans think is impossible.
This song contains a reference to Stagnant from Doki Doki Takeover Bad Ending. And it's not the only song in this mod that references it.
